Introduction

The sport Club Program at Lovely Professional University is comprised of sport clubs formed, developed, governed, and administrated by the student membership working in conjuction with the office of Intrammurals and Recreation, a unit of the Division of Student Life. The key to the success of this program is students leadership, interest, involvment and participation.
The Sport Club Council with advisement of the Office of Intramurals and Recreation has created this manual in order to provide officers with the structural Framework necessary for clubs to gain recognisation , devolep leadership skills. The policies and procedures contained herein apply to all the Clubs,Sport Club members, officers, coaches and the faculty/staff.





Defination Of a Sports Club

A sports club is defined as a registered student organisation that exists to promote and devolep interest in a particular sport or physical activity.
A clubs focus may be recreational, instructional, competitive, or a combination of these types of activites based on its constitution.
Sports Clubs are strictly voluntary and involvment in a Sport Club should enhance the student's collage experience and contributes to the student's overall education and wellness/p>



Purpose of a Sports Club

The Sports Club program at Lovely Proffesional University offers the community an oppurtunity to participate in a broad vareity of sports and recreation activities. All programs are based on student interest and designed in enhance the vertos experience by creating an environment where students can unite in diverse groups to acheive common goals and objectives while encouraging healthy life style. Sports Clubs provides a valuable learning experience through student involvment in fundraising,public relations, organisation, administration, budgeting, scheduling, teaching, and leadership devolepment.





Sports club council

the Sports Club Council(SCC) is primary,student-lead oversight committee responsible for advising, advocation and unifying all organisations within the Sports Clubs Program at Lovely Professional University and ensuring that participants are aware of their rights and responsibilities as Lovely Professiional University sports club studentathletes. The SCC will serve as the link in between sports Club and the collage administration, and be advised by the Director of Intramurals and Recreation. The SCC shall set and enforce the Sports Club polocies and procedures.





Responsibilities of Club

  • Follow the SCC constitutioon and by-laws
  • Conduct an SCC meating atleast once per month
  • Assist the Director of Intramurals and Recreationin conducting leadership training for sport club officers.
  • Recommend revisions of the Sports Club Manual and procedures for the following year.
  • Review and approve clubs seeking new admittance and/or reinstatment into the program.
